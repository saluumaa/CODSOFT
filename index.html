Biography

  • Zip2 (1995):
    Elon Musk co-founded Zip2, an online business directory, as an alternative to traditional paper yellow pages. The company was sold for over $300 million in 1999 to Compaq Computer Corporation.
  • X.com (1999):
    Musk invested part of his Zip2 windfall into X.com, an online bank he co-founded. X.com aimed to simplify digital fund transfers without traditional banking infrastructure. It later merged with another company to form PayPal.
  • PayPal (2000):
    PayPal, founded by Musk and others, became a secure online payments platform. Initially focused on payments for PalmPilots, it evolved into a global success. eBay acquired PayPal in 2002 for a $1.5 billion stock deal.
  • Tesla (2004):
    Musk joined electric car manufacturer Tesla as chairman of the board and later became CEO. Under his leadership, Tesla revolutionized the electric vehicle industry and expanded into energy storage and solar products.
  • SolarCity (2006):
    Musk co-founded SolarCity, a solar energy services company. The company aimed to make solar power more accessible to homeowners and businesses. Tesla eventually acquired SolarCity in 2016.
  • OpenAI (2015):
    Although not directly founded by Musk, he was one of the initial backers of OpenAI, an artificial intelligence research organization. OpenAI aims to create safe and beneficial AI for humanity.
  • Starship (2021):
    As part of SpaceX, Musk is leading the development of the Starship spacecraft. Starship aims to enable interplanetary travel, including missions to Mars.
